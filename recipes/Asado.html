Asado

Description

Make the best Argentinian Asado Ever !!!

Prepare Argentine roast:

  • 2 kg meat (a little strip roast, empty, roast lid, entrails, rump tail, steak, grill skirt or if you have a cut that you like, include it)
  • 4 chocolate black puddings (if it's one of the big ones, you break it up when you serve it)
  • 4 pork sausages
  • 2 kidneys
  • 2 kg chitterling
  • grill sausage
  • 1 free range chicken
  • Grill salt (my grandmother used coarse salt)
  • lemon for the chicken
  • Optional: Chimichurri
  1. Light the fire with wood or charcoal.
  2. Place the grill grates.
  3. The ideal is to cook the different pieces of meat according to their cooking time, as well as maintaining the texture of the pieces, once cooked.
  4. We will start by cooking the veal fillets, around 3 minutes on each side, without overcooking them.
  5. Then we will make the pork and lamb chops. In this case they are usually cooked a little longer, about four minutes, but without going over.
  6. Then we will gradually put the other pieces, sausages, black pudding, chorizo, chicken wings, etc. Around 6-7 minutes of cooking on each side will be ideal.
  7. It is interesting to sprinkle our Argentine roast with a generous portion of coarse salt.
