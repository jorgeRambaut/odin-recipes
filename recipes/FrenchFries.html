French Fries

Description

Make the best fries Ever !!!

  • 1 kilogram of potatoes
  • 3 liters of water
  • 1 liter per kilo of potato oil for frying
  • tablespoons cornstarch
  1. Peel the potatoes and cut them into canes, slices, cubes or as you like. Eye: it is important that they are of the same size so that the cooking is even
  2. Wash them in plenty of cold water several times to ensure that the potato releases all its juice and does not stick. Then, drain them for approximately 5 minutes and dry them with a (clean) kitchen towel to remove excess water.
  3. Place the potatoes washed and free of water in a bowl. This is the moment where we will add the 3 tablespoons of Cornstarch and mix well so that everything is well covered by the Cornstarch
  4. To fry it is important to use plenty of very hot oil (approx 170 / 180 Â°C)
